Falconer
A.K.A.
  • Info
  • |
  • Playstyle Tips
  • |
  • Loadout Tips

Ability
A Shock Drone that either attacks enemies or revive another dwarf.

has 5 charges, each taking 10 seconds to replenish.

The drone will search for any enemies near the pointed location and on the path to it.

The drone can be forced to target a specific enemy by pressing the [LMB] on said enemy.

Finding an enemy will result in the drone firing a barrage of 4 salvos,
each dealing 35 Damage on direct hit and 25 in an AoE.

Each barrage applies an electrocution effect and consumes 1 ability charge.

If it doesn't find any enemies, it will then be automatically recalled.

The drone can be manually recalled by holding [ABILITY] and then pressing [RMB].

Skills
Thunder Rod
Active:
Place a defensive zone that gives
+100% Electric Damage to allies within a 8m Radius.
Press [ABILITY] and then [USE] to place the Thunder Rod.

The Thunder Rod lasts for 15 seconds.
Cooldown: 120 Seconds.

Remote Drone Revive
Active:
The Shock Drone gains the ability to get a downed teammate back on their feet!

While looking at a downed teammate,
hold [ABILITY] and then [USE]
to order your Shock Drone to revive them.

Cooldown: 120 Seconds.

This is my favorite Class so far.

Since it does Electric damage it is a good idea to stack it.
What I was doing was getting Upgrades that made reduced my Ability cooldowns.
So i was just spamming my bird.

Ability
The Guardian's ability has 2 charges with a cooldown of 20 seconds each.
The ability has 2 different modes:

Repulsion Field [RMB]
Basically the opposite of Scorpion's Get Over Here!
They Repel enemies.
It will not not push away ReaperWorms .

Concussive Barrage [LMB]
shoots out 12 concussive munitions that damage and stun enemies
in a cone in front of the player,
each blast stuns the enemy for 6 seconds,
consuming 1 charge in the process

Skills

severely damaged Armor to allies within a 6m Radius.
Restores armor up to 50% of Max Armor Press [ABILITY] and then [USE] to place the Armor Beacon.
The Armor Beacon lasts for 15 seconds. Cooldown: 120 Seconds

Ability
designate a point and state in time by pressing [ABILITY] + [LMB],

this starts the Time Rewind which lasts for 20 seconds.

The point where it was initiated is marked by a distorted silhouette of her.

You can manually reactivate Rewind Time by pressing [ABILITY] + [RMB]
or after the 20 second duration is up.

Rewind Time automatically returns Retcon
to the location where the Time Rewind was initiated.
Any Ammo/Grenades, Health and Armor acquired or lost during the Time Rewind
is returned to exact values before the activation.

It seems like you can use it for Equipments like the turrets.
But seems to be bugged only could place down 4 turrets down at a time.

Skills
Rage
Active:
Accumulate Rage as you receive damage, and use it to give back two-fold!

Build up Rage by receiving damage.
When your Rage is full, press [ABILITY] + [USE] to deal
+100% Damage and gain +30%Pickaxe Speed for 12 Seconds.

Contingency Plan
Passive:
It's basically like Iron Will from Deep Rock Galactic.
But doesn't need Red Sugar to stay up.

It automatically revives Retcon when the last dwarf falls.
Revive with full Armor and Health.
Triggers once per mission.



In my opinion Retcon's Job/Playstyle is

I try to use the Rewind Time skill to revive,spam grenades, boss fights.

Ability
Shoot a crit dart that can be used in 2 different ways.

If an enemy is hit directly, they are marked for 5 seconds,
increasing the Crit Chance by +100% against it.

Shooting the ground leaves a marking zone that lasts for 10 seconds,
increasing Crit Chance against enemies inside the zone,
this effect lingers on the enemies after they leave it, although not for long.

Ability has 3 charges with a Cooldown of 18 seconds each.

Skills
Scanner
Passive:
Add a Sonar to your heads-up display.
The Sonar Radar will scan for enemies and friends
in a 35m Radius every 4 Seconds, and mark them as blips.

Active:
Press [ABILITY] and then [RELOAD]
to perform a Sonar Scan that highlights enemies and supplies for yourself and your friends.
Highlight lasts for 10 Seconds Cooldown: 24 Seconds

Ranger's Pocket
Active:
Press [ABILITY] and then [USE] to throw an Ammo Resupply Pack.
Resupplying recharges the Ranger's Pocket
