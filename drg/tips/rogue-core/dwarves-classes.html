Shock Drone
Either attacks enemies or revives another dwarf.

has 5 charges, each taking 10 seconds to replenish.

The drone will search for any enemies near the pointed location and on the path to it.

The drone can be forced to target a specific enemy by pressing the [LMB] on said enemy.

Finding an enemy will result in the drone firing a barrage of 4 salvos,
each dealing 35 Damage on direct hit and 25 in an AoE.

Each barrage applies an electrocution effect and consumes 1 ability charge.

If it doesn't find any enemies, it will then be automatically recalled.

The drone can be manually recalled by holding [ABILITY] and then pressing [RMB].

Ability
The Guardian's ability has 2 charges with a cooldown of 20 seconds each.
The ability has 2 different modes:

Repulsion Field [RMB]
Basically the opposite of Scorpion's Get Over Here!
It Repel enemies.

It will not not push away ReaperWorms .

Concussive Barrage [LMB]
Shoots out 12 concussive munitions that damage and stun enemies
in a cone in front of the player,
each blast stuns the enemy for 6 seconds,
consuming 1 charge in the process

Rewind Time
designate a point and state in time by pressing [ABILITY] + [LMB],

this starts the Time Rewind which lasts for 20 seconds.

The point where it was initiated is marked by a distorted silhouette of her.

You can manually reactivate Rewind Time by pressing [ABILITY] + [RMB]
or after the 20 second duration is up.

Rewind Time automatically returns Retcon
to the location where the Time Rewind was initiated.
Any Ammo/Grenades, Health and Armor acquired or lost during the Time Rewind
is returned to exact values before the activation.

It seems like you can use it for Equipments like the turrets.
But seems to be bugged only could place down 4 turrets down at a time

Crit Dart
Shoot a crit dart that can be used in 2 different ways.

If an enemy is hit directly, they are marked for 5 seconds,
increasing the Crit Chance by +100% against it.

Shooting the ground leaves a marking zone that lasts for 10 seconds,
increasing Crit Chance against enemies inside the zone,
this effect lingers on the enemies after they leave it, although not for long.

Ability has 3 charges with a Cooldown of 18 seconds each.
