Under the Dwarves (Classes) , I give tips and opinions on their "jobs".

You will have to get to Level 15,
Pay Merits .

Creatures / Enemies
Reaperworm
Reaperworms spawn when the Mission Timer basically hits zero.

They are tentacles that try to grab and kidnap the dwarves.

When you are grabbed teammates can shoot them to release the dwarf.

If you are totally grabbed, the next level/stage you must be rescued.

The other dwarves must release you from a cocoon.
There will be enemies surrounding the cocoons.
