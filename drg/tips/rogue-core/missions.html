General Tips

As of right now players can only join during the first level.

Once you get into the elevator to the next level nobody can join.


It seems the Ammo Resupply after getting off the Elevator
is always South West. 10+ times it happened.



You can tell which Boss will be at the end of the mission by the icon.
Gotoorak Molaktula Ramok
Gotoorak - looks like it's like T-Posing
Molaktula - looks like a M.
Ramok - Looks a Drill like it does spins moves.


Speaking of Bosses, Try to open Ammo Supplies before the boss fights.
Looks like there is at least 3 Ammo Supplies.


In Glacial Strata, on heavy snow, apparently you can run on the Big cable to run faster.

because sometimes i feel like it's faster and other times it doesn't feel faster?
