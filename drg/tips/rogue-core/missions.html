Gotoorak
Mutated Edgestalker looking thing.

Remember before the fight
Open all the Ammo Supplies and Upgrade if you have any.

You activate the Boss fight by touching the Floating Balls Orbs.

This boss is the most simplest and straight forward.

All you basically have to do is deplete it's shield health.

The Boss will jump and float in the middle of the giant hole
It is invincible and will shoot out a 360 attack
One of the Ball Orbs will come loose and
you chuck it in the giant hole.


Molaktula
Images
Remember before the fight
Open all the Ammo Supplies and Upgrade if you have any.

You activate the Boss fight by touching the Floating Balls Orbs.

If you damage the boss too much in rapid succession
The boss can fire a lot of mini missiles at the dwarves

The boss also has a stomping attack
That spawns spikes and can hit you.

After you deplete it's shield health

The Boss becomes invincible and everything goes into low gravity mode.

Then you must find these cocoons that hold crystals,
that you chuck into the giant hole.
Follow the red glowing lines.


Ramok
It kind of looks like a Beta Fish now that I look at the pictures.

Remember before the fight
Open all the Ammo Supplies and Upgrade if you have any.

You activate the Boss fight by touching the Floating Balls Orbs.

During the fight the boss will try to charge and ram.

As well as spin to suck the dwarves in or throw rocks.
You can shoot the flying rocks to stop them in their tracks.

Once you deplete it's shield health, in becomes invincible.

The Rift Stones will turn in Pillars
Which then you must go behind
and make the boss ram the pillars
to get the crystals, to throw in the pit.

Expenite Transmutation
Involves killing enemies within the marked area
The area is 10 meters wide.

Try not use Guardian's Repulsion skill
to drive away the enemies.

It's ideal to kill range enemies though
like Edgestalkers,Scorchers

Any enemies killed within that area
will turn into Expenite
in the form of a growing gem located in the center
with each enemy killed being worth 8 Expenite

A constant wave of enemies will spawn during the event,
with the event lasting for 70 seconds

The enemies killed in the area do not have to be ones triggered by the event to give Expenite;

they can originate from any swarm, allowing the player(s) to stack swarms for more Expenite Nodule.

Only 32 enemies must be killed
for the event to generate more Expenite
than any standard Expenite Nodule event
making it one of the most efficient methods
of gathering high amounts of Expenite.

Hard Rock Cracking
You can tell when the Rock Crackers need to be repaired,
By looking at the lights turning from greenish,orangish,yellowish,redish.

Pipe Connection
If you make a mistake building the pipes
you can pickaxe them to unbuild them.

Prospecting Drone Hunt
Involves the appearance of special drones which can be found wandering the caves.

Usually they are 3 drones to be find.

When idle, the Prospecting Drones will
wander around the caves aimlessly.

Once attacked, the Prospecting Drones will
run away from their attacker, and

begin spawning some defensive drones named DRG Sentry Bots.

These DRG Sentry Bot will attack players
by shooting rockets,
dealing considerable amounts of damage.

The DRG Sentry Bot will keep on spawning
as long as the Prospecting Drone is alive.

You can stun the Prospecting Drones

If you use Spotter's Crit Darts
They will start to attack as the darts do damage.

When a Prospecting Drones is destroyed,

players will be able to scan its data to find an Expenite Geode,

which is marked on the player(s) Terrain Scanner as a blue pickaxe.

This geode contains a huge vein of Expenite.

Ram Hammer Escort
It moves faster with more people in the radius.
If nobody is in the radius it stops.

Research Subject Elimination (Expenite Bulk Detonator)

Cameras

Data Terminals
Players are required to
have Clearance Level Alpha
to be able to interact with them.

When activated, two codes are displayed on the main screen.

Players must identify and select
the matching codes on the sides to retrieve the data.

If a wrong code is selected, the terminal will short circuit, requiring the players to retry with two different codes.

Once the correct matching codes are selected, the data will be added to the player(s) Terrain Scanner,

showing them
all events and points of interest,
such as Workbenches, Supply Pods, etc.

As of right now you don't have to match codes
to see the data on Terrain Scanner.
Only if you want credit for
the Reclaimer Points.
Maybe this is just a bug/not intended.

Gauntlets

Like Deep Dives in DRG,
You must use an Ascended (Promoted) Dwarf to participate.
Maps are Pre-Seeded.

in Gauntlets they are 7 levels of endless hordes of Corespawns.

You must kill certain number of enemies within a certain time limit before proceeding.

There is a bonus kill count which rewards a Workbench or Bio-Booster.
The Workbench or Bio-Booster will spawn in the elevator at the next stage.

You do not have to call the Elevator, it just opens when kill count is reached.
If you do not get to the elevator before the timer reaches Zero (0) then you fail.
Everybody has to be in the elevator to count, unless they are down.

You still need to mine Expenite for Upgrades.
Upgrades happen at the next stage.

Expenite in pockets carry over to the next stage,
but since Upgrades happen in the next stage
it is best to deposit right away before leaving.

You can sometimes find Expenite Gems in walls during runs.
Look for the green speckles on the walls.

The last level has no boss, and
has no need for Expenite,
it's just kill and get out!

A Drop Pod will descend when hitting the kill count.
