General Info-Tips

When playing with Green Beards I try to let them experience things
Like show them what a Compressed Gold , Jadiz etc. look like.
or what a cargo crate battery/helmet sounds like
Let them do some stuff like event hacking etc.


Disbanding the team will refresh the beer.


I Play by or try to either highest rank, or host buys the beer,
if neither has the bulbs and/or then the one with the most should pay for it.
But people are faster than I am and I can never buy beers!


Buy ALL Gear Modifications on your Armor for boosted Status
(Mastery, the Pill/Oblong icons at the bottom)


Each Dwarf is unique.
So I tend to look at each as they have different "Jobs" to do when playing.
Under the Dwarves (Classes) , I give tips and opinions on their "jobs".

Since Each Dwarf is unique.
It's suggested to get all of them Promoted once.
To see which one you like best.


To Promote a Dwarf,
You will have to get to Level 25,
then do a few missions, & then pay Credits /Minerals.
The Credits /Minerals increase the higher promoted rank you go up.
You do not have the play the same Dwarf you want to Promote.


When Hosting you can put it on Friends Only.
This prevents randoms from joining but
Allows people from Steam or Discord to join.

Communicate!
Communication is nice. Talk to your fellow Dwarves.
Whether it be in Voice or not

Communicate what builds you are bringing or want.

Make sure everybody is Ready before
pushing buttons to start things.

In chat [R] means Ready.

Also it could be taken that somebody is ready
if they Rock-N-Stone. ([V] Default)

Join Voice Chat first if you are in looking for group Discord.
Like the Official DRG Discord .

There is a Green Join button you can send to people
From the Official DRG Discord .
to set it up Follow the Pin Comments under
The Channel LFG Steam.

If the Join Button says Full/Game Ended and it's not
You can click on the Host's profile icon to refresh it
But like with everything Discord it can be bugged.
If the Official DRG Discord Invite doesn't work
Look at the Pin comments under the LFG groups.

Click on the person's profile icon to refresh.

You can get a Steam link to send to people.
Go to your Profile while in game.
Then look for a green Join Game button
Then Right-Click and click "Copy link address"


You can even join somebody else's game.
By clicking the Join Game button.

Also remember if you're in voice chat
Have some common courtesy

Don't have such an Open Mic
there is ways to get rid of noise.
Discord has Krisp Noise Suppression.
Under Voice & Video in Settings.

Rarity is a 1 in 21 mission (~4.76%) chance.

A chunk of Compressed Gold
is worth a random amount of gold units between 150-250
(awards at base 300-500 Credits and 300-500 Experience Points )

Rarity is a 1 in 3 mission.

Every 80 Nitra collected, a Resupply Pod can be called down.

A Matrix Core is a special resource primarily used to obtain
weapon overclocks and special cosmetics.

Players can obtain Matrix Cores from many sources,
most of which are unlocked after the first Promotion.

There are three main types of Matrix Cores:

Blank Matrix Core are the most common type of Matrix Core.
It has only one purpose: to be processed at a Core Infuser,
which consumes the Matrix Core Blank Core,

but in exchange produces an Infused Matrix Core or a
Mineral Container Mineral Container.

Core Infusers can be found at Machine Events,
only being accessible after the player has successfully completed the Machine Event.

Infused Matrix Core
Cosmetic Infused Cores
are used to craft Matrix Core Cosmetics.

Overclock Infused Cores
are used to craft unique weapon modifications
called Overclocks.

Check out Machine Events under Mission Tips.

Beer/Dwarf Roulette
Basically a drinking game you can play in the Space Rig.

Rules:
Basically everybody gets a Beer, then you all take turns Saluting ([V] Default),
Then who every get's the voice line with the name [Karl]
or you can play it where a word could be a Name in it .
drinks the beer.

The last person standing from drinking the beers wins.

Since each beer has a different potency,
depending on the beer you get you can have multiple chances.

When i first played it we just drunk the beer Blackout Stout.
So it's one drink and you are out!

If you do play multiple chances
I like the rule that only the person who speaks a Name/the Name Karl has to drink.
So somebody can last the whole entire time and not drink.
