Egg Hunts
Mission Overview
The primary objective is to collect a certain number of Alien Eggs .
As of right now there is 4, 6, & 8 Egg Hunts.

In Deep Dives there can be 4/6 as primary and 2 for secondary.



Mission Tips

There is a number of big swarms for an Egg mined.
4 Eggs - 1 of the 4 Eggs causes a big swarm.
6 Eggs - 2 of the 6 Eggs causes a big swarm.
8 Eggs - 3 of the 8 Eggs causes a big swarm.


On Hazard 5 there is the 2 minutes per egg rule.

because of how Egg Hunt missions spawn Nitra,
As Egg hunt has slightly less nitra spawns than the rest of the mission types.

So Sticking to 2 minutes per egg
(i.e. pulling an egg every two minutes).
This allows you to deal with the previous spawns, before getting more.
A good team can do 1 minute per egg.







Elimination (Dreadnoughts)
Mission Overview
In these missions have to locate and destroy multiple big boss bugs called Dreadnoughts .

The OG Dreadnaught, the Hiveguard, and the Twins.

Mission Tips
The OG Dread
Is the most straight forward to deal with.

It has Armor that you must break
before doing damage.

Once the armor is broken the Backside of the Dread will open up.
The Hiveguard
Before being able to damage the Hiveguard,
It spawns mobs called Sentinels
which need to be killed.

Then you must get rid of
Weak Points on the Hiveguard.

After that it's backside will open up.

Sometimes the game can bug out and take a while
to spawn the Sentinels or not at all
The Twins
As the name suggests it is 2 Dreads.

Their color's are kind of hard to tell apart.

I use a mod to change the colors
of the Arbalest to blue.
Better Contrasting Twins / Recolored Arbalest

Arbalest (Yellowish)
- Stays out of range like Climbs Ceilings.

Lacerator (Orangeish)
- Stays basically on the ground

They have no Armor, however

The heal each other.

When a Dreadnoughts spawns big swarms shouldn't spawn.
But smaller waves will occasionally spawn.
The Swarmer Modifier will keep spawning the little bastards.


Usually Spawn/Starting area is a good area to fight.


You can't have the same Dreadnought(s) spawn from the eggs back to back.
But you can have 2 of the same spawn on a 3 count.

So on a 3 Egg mission, You can have OG Dread, Twins, OG Dread
but cannot be OG Dread, OG Dread, Twins.


Start freezing the Dreadnoughts like quarter or less of their shield is down, not at full.
Also want to freeze it when the shield is broken and back is open.

The Drilldozer track is South West (225) on the compass. So you can Pre Drill if you want.

Drilldozer
- It goes faster when more dwarves on it

- Repairing with multiple people doesn't go faster.

- Fully repairing (When the health indicator shows white, not red),
Doretta briefly becomes invulnerable to all damage (~2 seconds)


At the second stage of the Ommoran Heartstone the Engineer can make
a Platform Bridge over the Drilldozer to protect from the slamming rocks.

You can start it early but don't go over too much or
it will be destroyed at the end of the stage


I classify this as an Exploit
and not Strategy like a Roof.

When the laser beam pillars spawn the Engineer
can bury Doretta's head in platforms
This prevents the laser beam pillars from spawning again.



When the laser beam pillars spawn,

The Scout can Zipline and pickaxe the tips
and the Engineer can help with platforms.

The Driller can use his drills to break them down.

The Gunner can use his weapons to gun them down,
like Autocannon, Mini Gun, or Rockets.


At the end of Escort Duty missions, you can try to go back to the start
to hopefully bring the Drop Pod closer to the others.
Usually send the Scout since he should be the fastest.

Industrial Sabotage
Mission Overview
Basically have to destroy this boss robot called the Caretaker .
Before you do it's protected by a force field and you must first
find two power stations, call down Hacking Pods and defend them.



Mission Tips

Be prepared for bots, and Fire works the best against them.


The Driller can dig above the Caretaker to throw C4 ontop of the Caretaker.

If you take 2,3, or 4 C4 you will basically
use all of them each Shieled (Yellow Health Bar) stage.
So The Driller will need a resupply for himself.


The Driller can make tunnels/bunkers to help hide in and move around.


The Engineer can make Platform Towers to help hide behind.

On-site Refining (Liquid Morkite)
Mission Overview
Like Point Extraction, there is a central platform and no M.U.L.E.
You must find 3 Pump Jacks and call them down for oil drilling
and then go the platform and build pipelines to them.




Mission Tips

Driller & Engineer are great here.


If you made a mistake building the Pipelines before building,

you can either
use the Pickaxe [Right Mouse Button] to deconstruct.
or damage the pipelines to undo it.
If you already built them you cannot undo them.



If there's an Event or something you want to grab do not fix the pipe lines
and let the bugs be busy with the broken pipelines.


When riding the Pipelines Hold [E] on a Pipeline

as it will slow down & stops at breakdown.


When riding the Pipelines Hold [Shift] on a Pipeline
to affix your screen to that direction.







Point Extraction
Mission Overview
Point Extraction mission is to collect glowing blue gems
embedded in the wall called an Aquarq .
They have small, blue crystal specks around the location.






Mission Tips

The mule isn't present on this mission.


Swarms become never ending,
so want to try to spend less than 15 minutes in the mission.

On Hazard 5, the infinite scaling kicks in at 12 minutes,
it becomes constant at 15, and unplayable at 20.


As bugs can attack through the metal mesh of the Mine Head,
it's not recommended to fight on it.

But you can try and fight around it because as it has Turrets
but the turrets have limited ammo.


Dwarves are instantly downed by the Mine Head if it lands on top of them,
like the Resupply Pods .










Salvage Operation
Mission Overview
You have to find a number of mini M.U.L.Es, and repair them with legs.

You then have to recall them back to the Drop Pod.

You then will have to fix an uplink box,

which once activated must stay in designated area until it finishes and then

Mission Control will spawn an Fuel Line Pod, which you must connect to the Drop Pod,
and again which once activated must stay in designated area until it finishes.
Then Drop Pod will open in about a minute and 30 seconds.



Mission Tips

Broken Resupply Pods can be found.
Which can be repaired After repairing them,
they have a chance to give 0 to 2 resupply racks
and up to 2 can be generated per mission

They appear almost completely buried in the ground,
with only a small portion sticking out.
Directly above them is a tunnel created by the resupply originally dropping in,
making those Resupply Pods highly noticeable thanks to the Terrain Scanner.


Repairing a Mini M.U.L.E. will reward the team with between
40-60 Nitra and 40-60 Gold.


Coming within 2 meters of a Mini M.U.L.E. for the first time
will trigger a large wave of enemies after 12 to 30 seconds.
Another, smaller wave of enemies will spawn
when one of the legs that are found near each
Mini M.U.L.E. is dug out. This leg is picked randomly.


When defending the Black Box or Fuel Lines
the Driller can drill out the walls
in like a crescent shape so the barrier fits
and so you can move around much better


Also the Engineer can use their Platforms to platform above the defending area
with the Repellant Additive modification, to help funnel bugs.


When defending the Black Box or Fuel Lines
and you call a Resupply Pod,
you can dig it down to stay out of your way.

Mission Overview

If you are in the Official DRG Discord

has a pin what the missions are.

Mission Tips

Deep Dives Hazard difficulty are
Deep Dive - 3, 3.5, 3.5
Elite Deep Dive - 4.5, 5, 5.5


Active Perks will reset between missions/stages, such as Field Medic or Iron Will .
You will be able to use the active abilities multiple times during the dive.


When playing solo, Bosco's revive charges will be reset between missions.

Machine Events Info
Machine Events are random events that spawn during missions.
They randomly spawn during regular missions but not Deep Dives.

Their purpose when beaten you can get either
Weapon Overclocks & Cosmetics Overclocks with things called Blank Cores.

When you get all Weapon Overclocks & Cosmetics Overclocks
You will start to get Mineral Cores.

You also get extra experience at the end of the mission.

As of right now there is 4 different Machine Events .
If already beaten, new players joining can't use their Blank Cores.

You don't have to complete Machine Events to get EXP.
So you can just start them up and let the timer run out.
Ebonite Mutation
Mission Overview

You use Melee attacks to
kill grey rocky versions of the bugs.

The machine will shoot out Power up items
(blue glowing canisters)
That you pick up by just running over them

so you can then power attack them
with little cooldown.

You can also just
regular pickaxe them as well.


Mission Tips

You can freeze/stun the Ebonite Creatures.



Kursite Infection
Mission Overview

These enemies are modified
with blue skin and yellow crystals.

They drop Yellow Crystals that have to be
deposited into the machine event.


Mission Tips

If it's on a ledge you can use
Engineer's Platforms
to make it bigger so the enemies
drops don't drop off the edge.

Omen Modular Exterminator
Mission Overview

This is the hardest machine event so far.
It will open a Tower which needs to be destroyed by exposing and shooting its green-colored weak spots.

To expose these weak spots, players will be required to stand on top of three different maintenance platforms around the tower for a certain amount of time.

Each platform will expose a different module's weak spot, which will be indicated in a pop-up display.

Each module contains multiple cooling tanks that each share an equal portion of it's health.


Mission Tips

Here is some tricks to make it easier.

The platform opposite of the Ignition Key Block is always the bottom of the Tower.
For the Radial Pulse-Gun

You can put a Resupply Pod
on the platform(s)
and stand on top of the them.

You can put Engineer's Platforms
over the platforms.
Basically the height of Molly is a correct height to build a bridge over them.

Speaking of Molly, she can actually sit on the platforms as well.

The Driller can dig trenches,
giving cover to the team.
