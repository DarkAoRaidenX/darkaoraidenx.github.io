Active Perks
+
Beast Master 🔗
Charm a Grunt , Grunt Guard , or Grunt Slasher
to become your loyal companion.
It is named Steeve .

Pros
+ It takes 0.75s to tame a Grunt.
+ Taming a Grunt fully heals them.

+ isn't affected by Sticky Flames from the
Driller's CRSPR Flamethrower .

+Steeve is affected by Mission Mutators ,

notably regenerating health with Regenerative Bugs , spawning a Parasite upon death with Parasites , and gaining bonus damage with Lethal Enemies .

Cons
-The tamed Grunt will always have the suffix (Steeve).
No offense to Steeve but custom names when?

-Health gets healed but armor stays broken.

-He is still vulnerable to weakspot damage bonuses
and armor breaking from the Dwarves.

Pros/Cons
Steeve has no target prioritization capability,
nor survival instincts,
being ignorant to any potential danger.

Which means he's fearless!
But at the same he's fearless...
+
Berzerker 🔗
+Go berzerk for 10 seconds during which you get
boosted melee damage
and a lightning-fast pickaxe Power Attack recharge.

Pros
+Boosts Power Attack cooldown rate:
+2100% of base cooldown rate.

+Increases the damage of regular Pickaxe strikes
by 8 while active.

Cons
-4 min cooldown. 2-3 minutes when??
-Damage increase does not provide any real benefit,
as it will not reduce the amount of strikes
needed to kill most creatures.
-Damage increase does not apply to
Power Attacks, only regular swings.
+
Dash 🔗
Propel forward with great speed!

Pros
+Movement Speed: x2.2
+Slowdown Reduction: 100%
(slow won't affect you at all).

+You can dash while holding heavy objects
(Aquaqs, Eggs, etc.).

Cons
-Duration: 1.5 seconds.
-Stun and Electricity slowdowns
don't get reduced.
-Can Dash to your death.
+
Field Medic 🔗
Help your fellow Dwarves up!
Once per mission,
you can get a downed teammate instantly!

Pros
+You revive teammates 30% faster.

+Instant Revive Button

+It will recharge at the beginning
of each stage of a Deep Dive.

Cons
-Instant Revive Button is
Once per mission.
-Sometimes can misfire the Instant Revive.
+
Heightened Senses 🔗
2 times per mission,
you can escape after being grabbed,
killing your captor in the process!.

Enemy Types that trigger's the alert
Cave Leech, Mactera Grabber, Nemesis, Nayaka Trawler.

Pros
+Instant Kills Enemies.

Cons
-Doesn't Instant Kill the Nemesis.
-Only can use 2 Times.
+
Hover Boots 🔗
Fancy boots that let you hover in midair
for 3.5 seconds.

Pros
+While active, you burn any creatures
right beneath you.

Cons
-Lasts only 3.5 seconds.
-does not immediately halt your momentum
(though it does reduce it very quickly).

As such, you can still slam into the ground
(taking fall damage)
if you are going extremely fast or activate it too late!.
+
Iron Will 🔗
Never give up!
Once per mission, get back into the fight!

Do not use it as soon as you go down!
Do not use it early in the mission!

Use it to either get Health, a Teammate up,
or make it to the Drop Pod.

Could use Iron Will to get closer to Teammates
if died some where up high but use at last resort.

As a Gunner try to use your shield
after you get Teammates up.

Pros
+When Active Immune to all damage.
+All weapons are reloaded upon entering Iron Will.
+Movement Speed: x1.2
+Base pickaxe damage: +2.5
+Power Attack cooldown rate:
+300% of base cooldown rate
+Slowdown reduction: 30%
+Obtaining health via any method during Iron Will will prevent the down from
occurring after the last stand duration.
+Works with Vampire.
+Dwarves under the effect of Iron Will count as alive for the purpose of mission extraction
+The single use active effect will recharge at the beginning of each stage of a Deep Dive.

Cons
-Shields do not regenerate.
-Pickaxe damage - Does not apply to power attacks.
-You can still be Cave-Leeched.
+
See You In Hell 🔗
You can trigger a large neuro-pulse explosion
after you go down.
A residual neuro-electric field will
electrocute and scare off enemies.

Pros
+Can Fear enemies and those usually immune to fear.
+It will recharge at the beginning
of each stage of a Deep Dive.

Cons
-Only 3 times each mission. 5 times when?
+
Shield Link 🔗
You can overcharge a teammate's shield to
300% for 30 seconds.

Pros
+Whenever you are within 4m of a teammate,
both of your shields recharge 50% faster!.
+Instantly regenerate all shield (Single Dwarf).


Cons
-Only works on a single Dwarf.
-Add 60 to the maximum shield value of the target
(unlike the description it doesn't really triple the shield value).
-If any damage is taken, the shield will only regenerate up to it's normal value,
any overshield used is lost forever..
-Neither the passive nor the active effect
of the perk can stack.
