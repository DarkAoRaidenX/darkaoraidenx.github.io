Playstyle Tips

I try to drill pathways to places & just make things more accessible
to like objectives or to get up places.

You can also tunnel under or to team mates to revive
or even to kill/hack enemies.

As well as even out the terrain for the team so it's easier to move around.

I am also always on the look out to get the buried Gems.
Like the Aquarqs, Bittergems, Gold Chunks, Jadizs, etc.

In boss fights like the Dreadnaughts I feel kind of useless. Our weapons don't do much except you can freeze them for a split second or 2 so the team can do damage but it takes a long time to freeze.

Equipment Tips

  • Cyro-Cannon |
  • Flamethrower |
  • Sludge Pump

  Cyro-Cannon is the best to me because it freezes enemies in their place
and flying enemies die right away when frozen.

which makes it easier to escape unlike the Flamethrower where it sets them on fire and they keep coming at you and the Flamethrower has to reload.

I Mainly use the Cyro-Cannon with the Wave Cooker
to Temperature Shock and the Liquid Cooling System Overclock.

or the Experimental Plasma Charger (EPC) with the Heavy Hitter Overclock
and then take the Grenade Impact Axes .

Cyro works well with the Vampire passive perk.
as The grenade Impact Axes and the Drills counts as melee damage.

Suggestions
MultiPlayer

I feel these are the most useful Active Perks in MultiPlayer.

Dash , Field Medic , & especially Iron Will in Deep Dives,
as it gives another chance to have successful missions.

I feel these are the most useful Passive Perks in MultiPlayer.

Resupplier , Thorns , & especially Vampire ,
as it works with the Iron Will Perk.


Solo

In my opinion these are most useful Active Perks for solo.

Beast Master , Dash , & especially Iron Will as it gives another chance to have successful missions.

In my opinion the most useful Passive Perks for solo.

Resupplier , Thorns , Vampire .

MODS

As of right now I am only using Quality of Life (QoL)/Visual/Audio Mods (Verified).

As of right now there is 3 category of mods. Approved, Sandbox, & Verified.

Approved: Not MultiPlayer friendly & Everybody has to install the mods.
Sandbox: Only can be used in a Sandbox saved game.
Verified: MultiPlayer friendly only effects the one who installs the mod.


Audio/Visual

Bosco + Weapon Paintjob Unlocker
Allows you to apply DLC, Armor, Bosco, and Pickaxe paintjobs to your favorite firearms. Vice versa with Pickaxes and Bosco.

Blue Flamethrower
Turns the color of the Driller's CRSPR Flamethrower flames into blue.

Blue Minigun Muzzle Flash + Tracers
Changes the color of the Lead Storm's muzzle flash and tracers to blue, for that laser minigun feel.

Customizable Ally Outlines
Allows you to change the color and duration of outlines in real time per character class and other NPCs. Wish the game added a Green Color.

Customizable HUD + Crosshair
Make the vanilla HUD your own!

Doom Eternal Shield Sound
Basically, it sounds meatier.

Gears of War Sound for Leadstorm Minigun
Changes the sounds for the Leadstorm minigun to those used by the Mulcher from the Gears of War Franchise.

Glowing Equipment
Make things like the M.U.L.E and Resupply Pod glow in the dark!

Glowing Lines
this makes Zip Lines glow green like the Gunner and Grappling Hook lines glow blue like the Scout.

Modified Gunner Shield Effect
Makes the Gunner Shields easier to see through & looks very bad ass!

Sticky Flame Status Icons
This mod enables the CRSPR Flamethrower's sticky flames damage and slowdown to pop up with icons on enemy healthbars.

WoT Large Cannon Firing Sound for Autocannon
Changes the Autocannon's firing sound to World of Tanks' large cannon firing sound.

Misc

Build Inspector
Lets you view the builds of everyone in the lobby.

Closer Promotion Terminal
For the lazy, this mod adds a promotion terminal to your personal cabin.

Fast Forging
Another one for the lazy ones, including me! It skips the overclock forging animation.

MOTD + Stat Track
Automatically posts a message in chat whenever a person joins your lobby. Stat Track tracks player kills, assists and damage for each enemy type

Rancor's Rig HUD
It will show a summary of such missions on the side of the screen while in the space rig and the current daily special beer and the mission rotation timer.

Show More Promotion Levels
Show how many times you or your fellow Dwarves promoted after Legendary!

Now you'll be able to find the greyest of the greybeards!

SimpleQOL
A collection of tweaks and QOL improvements to make DRG that much more fun to play!

Quality of Life (QoL)

A Better Modding Menu
Adds a Mod Filter and you can copy and share your installed mods.

Ammo Percentage Indicator
Displays the currently equipped weapon's ammo as a percentage on the screen.

Ammo Resupply Indicator
Indicates when ammo is low enough to resupply without overflowing. As A single resupply will restore up to 50% ammo.

Better Contrasting Twins / Recolored Arbalest
This mod recolors the Arbalest to be blue rather than yellow, which makes it much easier to tell apart from its orange comrade.

Better Downed Icon
Removes the "X" overlay from the downed icons, allowing you to actually see which dwarf class the downed player is using

Better Explosion Range Indicator
Driller's Satchel Charge (C4) explosion range sphere redesigned and new features added for better visibility and clarity.

Better Spectator
Improved spectator camera while downed. Lets you freely switch between all players as well as Bosco, Molly, Doretta, and BET-C and freely rotate and zoom around them.

Better Unknown Horror Indicator
The material of Unknown Horror has been redesign to improve visibility and implement a color distance indicator.

Brighter Objects
Visibility of almost all resources has been increased. For now I disabled this to give back the challenge of finding these.

Combined Resources
See all of your fellow Dwarves resources combined. No longer need to wonder what needs to be mined!

Display Stabber Vines' HP
shows the HP bar of the Stabber Vines in Hollow Bough.

Health and Shield Numbers
Adds a numeric representation of health and shields to the HUD. Why isn't this in the base game?!

Mod Hub
A centralized hub for modding user interfaces.

Platform Placement Preview
Adds a preview for platform placement similar to sentry turrets and resupply pods.

Steeve Better Visibility
To avoid the pain and suffer, Steeve's visibility is increased drastically. His/her heart particles can be spotted far away even in big chaotic fights.

Vanity Search
Adds a search box to the wardrobe.

Weapon Heat Crosshair
Adds a preview for platform placement similar to sentry turrets and resupply pods.
