General Info-Tips
- Each Dwarf is unique.
So I tend to look at each as they have different "Jobs" to do when playing.
Under the Dwarves (Classes) tab, I give tips and opinions on their "jobs".

- Since Each Dwarf is unique.
It's suggested to get all of them Promoted once.
To see which one you like best.

To Promote a Dwarf,
You will have to get to Level 25,
then do a few missions, & then pay Credits/Minerals.
The Credits/Minerals increase the higher promoted rank you go up.
You do not have the play the same Dwarf you want to Promote.

- Communication is nice.
Talk to your fellow dwarves.

Speaking of which...

- Don't double dip from the Resupply Pod
and as well as don't give away somebody else's Resupply.

- There is friendly fire!

- "R" means Ready.

- Buy ALL Gear Modifications on your Armor for boosted Status
(Mastery, the Pill/Oblong icons at the bottom)

Driller

"Dirt" Digger, The DrillDozer, Terrorist, Yellow One

  • |

In my opinion Driller's Job/Playstyle is

As Driller is one I play the most
it annoys me when people barely use their Drills in missions.
You have have Drills for a reason...

I try to think what would be the easiest way to go
and what would be easier for my Teammates.

I try to drill pathways to places & just make things more accessible,
to like objectives, supply pods or to get up to places.

You can also tunnel under or to team mates to revive
or even to kill/hack enemies.

As well as even out the terrain for the team so it's easier to move around.

I am also always on the look out to get the buried Gems.
Like the Aquarqs, Bittergems, Gold Chunks, Jadizs, etc.

Pressing "R" when having the Drills out, cools them down faster.

Cyro-Cannon is the best to me because it freezes enemies in their place
and flying enemies die right away when frozen.

which makes it easier to escape
unlike the Flamethrower where it sets them on fire and
they keep coming at you and the Flamethrower has to reload.

I Mainly use the Cyro-Cannon with the Wave Cooker
to Temperature Shock and the Liquid Cooling System Overclock.

or the Experimental Plasma Charger (EPC)
with the Heavy Hitter Overclock
and then take the Grenade Impact Axes .

Cyro works well with the Vampire passive perk.
as The grenade Impact Axes and the Drills counts as melee damage.

In my opinion Engineer's Job/Playstyle is

As an Engineer you basically place Cheese/Platforms, around and under minerals for mostly the Scout to get to.

You can also place the Platforms to plug holes in the roof or floor and to get over stuff like Sticky Goo or Lava Craters. Also don't forget to place down your Turrets.

In game there's basically stop/break points where you can set up your turrets, like during Egg Hunt missions whenever you take out an egg there's a chance to have a swarm incoming.

Speaking of swarms The SSG(Swarm Shredders) Grenades are basically one the best grenades in the game right now.

General Mission Tips
Other Dwarves can build the Engineers turrets.

When being yoinked by the Glyphid Stingtail
and it's a steep fall try to aim for it's body to land on it, to prevent fall damage.
Egg Hunts
As of right now there is 4, 6, & 8 Egg Hunts
each one has a number of Big Swarms.
4 has 1 Big Swarm, 6 has 2 Big Swarms, and 8 has 3 Big Swarms.
Elimination (Dreadnaughts)
When a Dreadnaught spawns regular waves shouldn't spawn.
The Swarmer Modifier will keep spawning the little bastards.

You can't have the same Dreadnaught(s) spawn from the eggs back to back.
But you can have 2 of the same spawn on a 3 count.
Escort Duty
Drilldozer
- It goes faster when more dwarves on it
- Repairing the Drilldozer/Dorrieta/Dotty with multiple people doesn't go faster.

When the laser beam pillars spawn, the Engineer(NG) can platform the tips so the scout can get on them, the Driller can drill them down, or Gunner can use like the mini gun to break the tips
Industrial Sabotage
The Driller can dig above the Caretaker or
use the largest area on their C4 to blow up the shields.
Mining Expedition (Morkite)
The Drop Pod always drops in the North East direction.

Point Extraction
Swarms become never ending, so want to try to spend less than 15 minutes in the mission

On-site Refining (Liquid Morkite)
If there's an Event or something you want to grab do not fix the pipe lines
and let the bugs be busy with the broken pipelines.

When riding the Pipelines Hold "E" on a Pipeline
as it will slow down & stops at breakdown.

When riding the Pipelines Hold "Shift" on a Pipeline
to affix your screen to that direction.
Salvage Operation
Repairing a Mini M.U.L.E. will reward the team with between
40-60 Nitra and 40-60 Gold.

Coming within 2 meters of a Mini M.U.L.E. for the first time will trigger a large wave of enemies after 12 to 30 seconds. Another, smaller wave of enemies will spawn when one of the legs that are found near each Mini M.U.L.E. is dug out. This leg is picked randomly.

When defending the Black Box or Fuel Lines
the Driller can drill out the walls in like a crescent shape so the barrier fits and so you can move around much better

Also the Engineer can use their Platforms to platform above the defending area with the Repellant Additive modification, to help funnel bugs.

When defending the Black Box or Fuel Lines
and you call a Resupply Pod,
you can dig it down to stay out of your way.
Deep Dives
Deep Dives Hazard difficulty are
Deep Dive - 3, 3.5, 3.5
Elite Deep Dive - 4.5, 5, 5.5

Active Perks will reset between missions, such as Field Medic or Iron Will
will be able to use their active abilities multiple times during the dive.

When playing solo, Bosco's revive charges will be reset between missions.
Machine Events
As of right now there is 4 different Machine Events.
They randomly spawn during regular missions but not Deep Dives.

You don't have to complete Machine Events to get EXP.
So you can just start them up and let the timer run out.

MODS
As of right now I am only using Quality of Life (QoL)/Visual/Audio Mods (Verified).

As of right now there is 3 category of mods. Approved , Sandbox, & Verified.

Approved: Not MultiPlayer friendly & Everybody has to install the mods.
Sandbox: Only can be used in a Sandbox saved game.
Verified: MultiPlayer friendly only effects the one who installs the mod.
