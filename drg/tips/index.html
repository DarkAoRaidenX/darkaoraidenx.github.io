General Info-Tips

When playing with green beards I try to let them experience things
Like show them what a Compressed Gold , Jadiz etc. look like.
or what a cargo crate battery/helmet sounds like
Let them do some stuff like event hacking etc.


I Play by the rule or try to either highest rank, or host buys the beer,
if neither has the bulbs then the one with the most should pay for it.


There is Friendly Fire and Fall Damage.
Friendly Fire isn't as bad as I thought when first starting
But Fall Damage can be unforgiven even on Hazard 1 (Lowest Difficulty).


Each Dwarf is unique.
So I tend to look at each as they have different "Jobs" to do when playing.
Under the Dwarves (Classes) tab, I give tips and opinions on their "jobs".

Since Each Dwarf is unique.
It's suggested to get all of them Promoted once.
To see which one you like best.


To Promote a Dwarf,
You will have to get to Level 25,
then do a few missions, & then pay Credits/Minerals.
The Credits/Minerals increase the higher promoted rank you go up.
You do not have the play the same Dwarf you want to Promote.


Don't double dip from the Resupply Pod
and as well as don't give away somebody else's Resupply.


Communication is nice. Talk to your fellow Dwarves.

Join Voice Chat first if you are in looking for group Discord.
Like the Official DRG Discord .

There is a Green Join button you can send to people
From the Official DRG Discord .
to set it up Follow the Pin Comments under
The Channel LFG-Steam.

If the Join Button says Full/Game Ended and it's not
You can click on the Host's profile icon to refresh it
But like with everything Discord it can be bugged.

Also remember if you're in voice chat
Have some common courtesy

Don't have such an Open Mic
there is ways to get rid of noise.
Discord has Krisp Noise Suppression.
Under Voice & Video in Settings.


When Hosting you can put it on Friends Only.
This prevents randoms from joining but
Allows people from Steam or Discord to join.


Disbanding the team will refresh the beer.


[R] means Ready.


Buy ALL Gear Modifications on your Armor for boosted Status
(Mastery, the Pill/Oblong icons at the bottom)

Beer/Dwarf Roulette
Basically a drinking game you can play in the Space Rig.

Rules:
Basically everybody gets a Beer, then you all take turns Saluting ([V] Default ),
Then who every get's the voice line with the name [Karl]
or you can play it where a word could be a Name in it .
drinks the beer.

The last person standing from drinking the beers wins.

Since each beer has a different potency,
depending on the beer you get you can have multiple chances.

When i first played it we just drunk the beer Blackout Stout.
So it's one drink and you are out!

If you do play multiple chances
I like the rule that only the person who speaks a Name/the Name Karl has to drink.

General Mission Tips

There is a compass on the top center of the screen.
You might have to turn it on in the settings.
I am surprised a lot people didn't know this even Grey Beards.


Pressing [Space] when looking at the Terrain Scanner,
fixes your character into the direction you're looking at.
Useful when Drilling.

You can also just rotate the Terrain Scanner Map.
Apparently some did not know this.


Should Ping with the Laser Pointer [CTRL]
Boss Enemies often for the Team.


If you go down, you can use your camera to help your Teammates!
Use this time to call out enemies, find objects, health etc.

Also when you get revived you'll get up facing the direction you was looking.


You can use a Dwarf's head to bounce off of to prevent Fall Damage.


When falling from heights
You can hold [E] to grab a hold of things
like the Gunner's Ziplines or Pipelines.


You can help out Engineers by
Helping build their turrets.

Also other Engineers can Refill each others turrets.


When Frozen you can Pickaxe/Power Attack a Dwarf to free them.


You can throw the heavy objects like the Enor Pearl ,
basically anything you can carry to go faster.


The Glyphid Praetorian , Glyphid Oppressor
release a Green Death Gas which can be lit on fire with any Heat Source to get rid of it
but it will explode so watch out.


When Mining Minerals you should keep minining
So the minerals can be already mined or
They can fall down from high places

So your fellow dwarves can pick them up
Also don't have to come back and mine
You can just pick them up easier when coming back.


If you make a mistake with Rock Cracker Pods Cables
you can pickaxe them break to redo them.


When cleaning up a Lithophage Outbreak
you will want to Foam everything at once and then Vacuum the area.
This way you won't have to deal with multi waves spawning from the area.


When being yoinked by the Glyphid Stingtail
and it's a steep fall try to aim for it's body to land on it, to prevent fall damage.

Seems to work with any large bug like the Praetorian.


You can hit or shoot the Glow Trees Bulbs to light up the area.
Hitting all five (5) bulbs will make the tree light up permanently.


At the end of a mission Follow the Driller!
Unless you're a Scout, you can just do Scout things.


At the end of the mission if whoever gets to the Drop Pod first
Should Ping the Drop Pod at the end.
Especially helps the Driller know where to drill.


You only have to deposit mission objectives for it to count.

Salvage Operation
Mission Overview
You have to find a number of mini M.U.L.Es, and repair them with legs.

You then have to recall them back to the Drop Pod.

You then will have to fix an uplink box,

which once activated must stay in designated area until it finishes and then

Mission Control will spawn an Fuel Line Pod, which you must connect to the Drop Pod,
and again which once activated must stay in designated area until it finishes.
Then Drop Pod will open in about a minute and 30 seconds.

Mission Tips

Broken Resupply Pods can be found.
Which can be repaired After repairing them,
they have a chance to give 0 to 2 resupply racks
and up to 2 can be generated per mission

They appear almost completely buried in the ground,
with only a small portion sticking out.
Directly above them is a tunnel created by the resupply originally dropping in,
making those resupply pods highly noticeable thanks to the Terrain Scanner.


Repairing a Mini M.U.L.E. will reward the team with between
40-60 Nitra and 40-60 Gold.


Coming within 2 meters of a Mini M.U.L.E. for the first time
will trigger a large wave of enemies after 12 to 30 seconds.
Another, smaller wave of enemies will spawn
when one of the legs that are found near each
Mini M.U.L.E. is dug out. This leg is picked randomly.


When defending the Black Box or Fuel Lines
the Driller can drill out the walls
in like a crescent shape so the barrier fits
and so you can move around much better


Also the Engineer can use their Platforms to platform above the defending area
with the Repellant Additive modification, to help funnel bugs.


When defending the Black Box or Fuel Lines
and you call a Resupply Pod,
you can dig it down to stay out of your way.
