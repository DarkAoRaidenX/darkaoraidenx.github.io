 		<br><br>
									A method/exploit of bringing the <a title="DRG Wiki" class="reg-link" href="https://deeprockgalactic.wiki.gg/wiki/Drop_Pod" target="_blank">Drop Pod</a> closer,
									
									<br>
									is to mine down/make a hole, 
									
									then get everyone as well as <a title="DRG Wiki" class="reg-link" href="https://deeprockgalactic.wiki.gg/wiki/M.U.L.E." target="_blank">Molly</a> in the hole, 
									<br>
									platform over the hole, and then call for it. 
									<br>
									
									<span class="highlights">Do Not</span> dig too close to a cave or <a title="DRG Wiki" class="reg-link" href="https://deeprockgalactic.wiki.gg/wiki/M.U.L.E." target="_blank">Molly</a> will phase through the terrain.

									<br><br>
									This works because <a title="DRG Wiki" class="reg-link" href="https://deeprockgalactic.wiki.gg/wiki/M.U.L.E." target="_blank">Molly</a> is stuck and cannot get to the <a title="DRG Wiki" class="reg-link" href="https://deeprockgalactic.wiki.gg/wiki/Drop_Pod" target="_blank">Drop Pod</a> as intended 
									<br>
									so the game spawns it closer to the Dwarves.
